About Auto-Hide Rules

Auto-hide rules help you automatically hide events when they overlap with other events. For example:

  • Hide "On-call" events when they overlap with "Vacation" events
  • Hide "Meeting" events when they overlap with "Out of Office" events

Use regex patterns to match event summaries. The "Hide Pattern" determines which event to hide ("1" or "2").
